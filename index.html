About Us

We South Eastern Kenya University Christian Union, a body of christian students of SEKU bound together in the common faith in the only one God are:

  • Joint together with a heavenly calling of Jesus Christ
  • Committed to living according to the Holy Scripture
  • Exclusively non-political and non-denomination

Our Objectives

  1. Leadership development:

    Developing members of the union in their social and Leadership skills

  2. Discipleship:

    Challenging and deepening the faith of members by teaching, prayers, bible-study and worship. This will also include encouraging members to present Jesus Christ to others and give them an opportunity to make a personnal commitment with them.

  3. Evangelism:

    To sensitize members into mission work in every area of life to which God calls them. This will also include individually and cooperatively proclaiming the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to our fellow students and living life consistent with the gospel.

Mission

To proclaim the Gospel in SEKU, the immediate region, around Kenya and the entire world and to nature people to grow spiritually, socially, morally, academically, professionally, talent-wise and leadership-wise

Vision

To be a family of well nurtured resonsible Christians having Godly impact in the campus and the society.

Core Values

  • Holiness
  • Faithfulness to the Holy Scripture
  • Unity
  • Integrity
